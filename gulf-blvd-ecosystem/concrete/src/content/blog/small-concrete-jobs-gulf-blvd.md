Most concrete projects on Gulf Blvd aren't dramatic full replacements — they're smaller, practical jobs that make a property safer, more functional, or better maintained. We handle projects of all sizes. Here's what's most common and what to expect for cost.

## Common Small Concrete Jobs on Gulf Blvd

**Crack repair:** Surface crack injection with polyurethane or epoxy filler, followed by sealer application over the repaired area. Typical cost: $150–$500 depending on number and length of cracks. For structural cracks (settlement-related), more extensive assessment and repair may be needed.

**HVAC and generator pads:** Equipment pads for AC condensers, heat pumps, and whole-house generators need to be level, properly sized, and structurally adequate to support the equipment. These are typically 3'×3' to 4'×6' slabs, often elevated slightly for drainage clearance. Cost: $300–$800.

**Outdoor shower base:** Pinellas County beach properties almost universally need outdoor shower areas. A concrete shower base needs proper drainage (typically a center drain or slope to a drain at one edge), excellent slip resistance, and durable sealing. Cost: $400–$900 depending on size and drain complexity.

**Walkway section replacement:** Individual sections of a concrete walkway that have settled, cracked badly, or heaved from root intrusion. We saw-cut and replace the affected section. Cost: $200–$600 per section.

**Step replacement or repair:** Front entry steps and pool area steps crack and spall with age, especially in coastal Florida. New concrete steps or step overlays restore function and appearance. Cost: $400–$1,200 for 2–3 steps.

**Pool equipment pad:** Concrete pad for pool pumps, filters, and heaters. Needs to be level, resistant to pool chemicals, and properly positioned for equipment access. Cost: $400–$700.

**Mailbox pad:** A small concrete pad for a post-mounted mailbox. Protects the post base from rot (relevant in Florida's wet soil conditions). Cost: $150–$300.

**Concrete curbing/edging:** Continuous formed concrete edging to define landscape beds, driveways, or pool areas. Cost: $8–$15 per linear foot.

## Minimum Project Sizes

Small concrete jobs involve the same mobilization cost as large ones — truck time, crew time, tool setup. For projects under $400–$500 in material cost, the mobilization component makes per-unit cost high. We still do small jobs, but understand that the $/sq ft for a 20 sq ft pad will be higher than for a 200 sq ft patio.

If you have multiple small projects, grouping them into a single visit reduces total cost per project.

## Getting an Estimate for Small Work

We provide written estimates for projects of all sizes. For small repairs (cracks, single slab sections), a brief site visit is usually enough to assess and price. For more complex small projects (drainage, step replacement), we spend more time on assessment.
